Shalom Lekoulam! Or alternatively, Hi Everyone!

We are a team of four students at MIT who are spending our January Independent Activities Period in Kibbutz Ein Shemer, Israel, teaching high school! We will be utlizing the community greenhouse to perform science experiments, teaching English via science articles, building aeroponic and hydroponic farming systems, modeling computational thinking with mechanical bit-adder systems, and exploring Israel in our free time. We are very excited and hope you will follow our adventures as we share and post about them here!

Sincerely,
Margalit, Nikhil, Emmanuel, Meia

PS: Questions, Comments, Concerns, Inquiries? Feel free to shoot us an email!
